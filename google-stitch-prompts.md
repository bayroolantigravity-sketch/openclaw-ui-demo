# OpenClaw UI - Google Stitch / AI Prompt Guide
*(Luxurious, Monochromatic, High-End Editorial Fashion App Mockups)*

This document contains specialized prompt engineering prompts designed to instruct AI image generators (like Google Stitch, Midjourney, or DALL-E) to recreate the aesthetic feel of the OpenClaw UI application.

## Core Aesthetic Directives
Always include these keywords in your prompts to maintain the design system:
> **Core Style:** High-end editorial fashion app, luxury brand aesthetic, SSENSE/Vogue inspired, stark monochrome UI, minimalistic layout, high-contrast black and white, subtle translucent silver accents, glassmorphism, sharp contemporary corners.
> **Typography:** Playfair Display (Serif), Space Grotesk (Sans-serif), elegant editorial typography.
> **Vibe:** Cinematic, moody, premium, photorealistic, 8k, dribbble award-winning design.

---

## Screen-Specific Prompts

### 1. Landing Page / Hero Section
**Prompt:**
> A hyper-realistic, high-end editorial mockup of a smartphone displaying a luxury fashion app landing page. The app interface is stark black and white, minimalist, featuring sophisticated typography. On the screen, there is a bold, elegant serif headline and a high-fashion editorial product image. The background of the mockup is a dark, sleek ambient environment with a subtle metallic silver aurora effect. Cinematic studio lighting, 8k resolution, photorealistic, Vogue editorial aesthetic, dribbble top UI design.

### 2. The Style Quiz (Image Selection)
**Prompt:**
> A premium UI/UX mockup of a fashion styling app on an iPhone screen, viewed at a slight dynamic angle. The screen shows an interactive fashion quiz. The interface is monochromatic, utilizing stark black, pure white, and minimal greys. The screen displays a grid of beautiful, aesthetic fashion photography inside perfectly proportioned vertical cards (aspect ratio 4:5). Minimalist black accent checkboxes and elegant serif font questions. The phone is floating against a soft, dark studio background with subtle soft-box lighting. 3D render, highly detailed, luxury monochrome aesthetic.

### 3. The Product Feed (Daily Drop)
**Prompt:**
> A close-up, high-end presentation mockup of a mobile fashion app personalized product feed. The UI design resembles a luxury fashion magazine layout. It features a stunning, high-contrast monochrome color palette, sharp corners, and glassmorphic translucent elements on the action buttons. The app displays high-quality designer clothing items (e.g., leather jackets, avant-garde sneakers) on stark backgrounds. Premium typography with minimalist "Match" badges. Suspended in mid-air over a matte dark gray surface, hyper-detailed, luxury brand aesthetic.

### 4. Lifestyle / "In-Use" Context
**Prompt:**
> A cinematic fashion photography shot of an elegant person holding a smartphone, actively using a luxury fashion styling app. The app on the screen is clearly visible: a dark mode, highly minimalist fashion feed interface with editorial typography. The person is dressed in chic, contemporary designer clothing (black tones). Shot in a dimly lit, upscale, modern architectural space. Moody lighting, depth of field, 85mm lens, photorealistic.
